### Tips for Usage

1.  **Don't overdo it**: Too many emoji can make text hard to read.
2.  **Cross-platform check**: Emoji look different on Windows, macOS, and Android. Ensure the meaning is preserved.
3.  **Accessibility**: Screen readers read the emoji name (e.g., "Red Circle"). Use them as decoration or ensure the text explains the meaning too.
